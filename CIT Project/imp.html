The Importance of Quantum Processing

 

 

The technology used in computers today connect us, inspire us, and give us the ability to express endless creativity.

 

This technology is limited though, being only able to do what we tell them to do in 1s and 0s or similar to the head and tails of a coin, an example used by IBM.

 

Quantum Processing brings a new kind of processing which spins the “coin”.

 

This allows for solutions to large amounts of data in very complex systems.
